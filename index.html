You play DRUMS!

You are a highly skilled musician, and most people don't even notice because you look so cool. Nobody in the band is going to fuck with you because you are in control of the time and nobody wins when the drummer is mad. You probably have good hair.

You play KEYBOARDS!

All of those years of piano lessons have payed off, and now you get to tour around and play easy parts you could do in your sleep. Your bandmates will ask you to referee musical questions, but you don't like being the center of attention. You like wearing sunglasses on stage so that people in the audience can't see how bored you are.

You play VIOLIN!

All those years of lessons and here you are to play the intro on a couple of songs. You don't tell people you play in a rock band because they look so disappointed when you tell them what you play. Even you think you can be replaced by a sample played from a laptop.

You play RHYTHM GUITAR!

Listen, there's only room for one guitar solo chasing diva in a band, and it didn't end up to be you. That's ok, though, playing in perfect time with drums and bass is harder than it looks, and your midlife crisis is going to be 80% less embarassing. Also, none of your bandmates will figure out that you own the copyright to all of your songs for another 10 years.

You play BASS!

You're the person in the band who holds down the bottom. Keeping good time with the drums isn't easy, but let's be real: you play one note at a time on an instrument with only four strings. You don't stand out on stage but look cool anytime someone looks over to you. You are probably bisexual, and none of your bandmates realize that you run the instagram page and all of their social media fans are really your fans.

You play LEAD GUITAR!

Your favorite things to say are "Can you turn me up?" and "I think this would be a great place for a guitar solo." You watched A Goofy Movie when you were four years old and thought Max was the coolest in the world. You decided you were going to play guitar, and you never revisited that dream. Your pal who plays rhythm guitar is probably a better player but you have more pedals.

You are the LEAD SINGER!

You are the face of the band. You like answering questions about the deep meaning of your songs, even though technically speaking all of your songs are written by the bass player. You're not so big on "practicing" or "counting" or "getting the right notes," but the fans are used to seeing you so it's too late to make any changes.

You are the WEIRD PERSON IN THE BACK WITH A LAPTOP!

You didn't get into music to set up laptops on stage and click a mouse to trigger samples. Unfortunately, you were not very good at performing live. Since you were better at using computers than the rest of your bandmates anyway, when it came time to set up the tour this was the spot you were given. At least this way you can pretend to be a DJ on Tinder.

You play the SAXOPHONE!

Ten years ago the band had a radio hit with an ironic saxophone solo. Little did they know that they would have to unironically hire a sax player to go on tour with them for the rest of their career. Your best friend is the person who plays handbells on the other big hit, you both have a lot of downtime.
